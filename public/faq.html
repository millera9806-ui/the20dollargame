FAQ

How does the game work?

Once per day, the live draw opens at 7 PM PST. The entry window stays open for 2 minutes. Anyone who enters during that window is included, and one winner is selected at random after the timer ends.

How do I win?

Enter during the 2-minute live window. Speed does not matter. Everyone who enters during that window has the same chance to win when the random draw happens.

What time does the drop happen?

The daily draw goes live at 7 PM PST.

How long is the entry window?

The entry window is open for 2 minutes once the draw goes live.

Is it free?

Yes. No purchase necessary to enter or win.

Why do you give away $20 every day?

Why not? It is fun, chaotic, and gives someone a little cash daily.

Will my information be stored?

Basic claim information for entries and winners is stored so payouts can be verified and past winners can be tracked in the admin dashboard.
