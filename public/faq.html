FAQ

How does the game work?

Each day, a 1-minute claim window opens. During that window, anyone on the site can hit the button to try and grab $20. The first submission wins — simple as that.

How do I win?

Be online when the window opens, fill in your payout info, and hit “Gimme That $20” fast. First come, first served.

Why do you give away $20 every day?

Why not? It’s fun, chaotic, and gives someone a little cash daily.

Will my information be stored?

Nope. Only the basic claim info for the daily winner is logged to verify payout.
