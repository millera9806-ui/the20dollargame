Frequently Asked Questions

How does the game work?
A random one-minute window opens each day. During that time, a $20 prize is up for grabs — first person to submit a valid claim wins. 🤑

How do I win?
Be ready! Once the prompt appears, enter your payout info and hit “Gimme that $20” before anyone else does.

Why do you give away $20 every day?
Why not? It’s fun, random, and keeps things exciting. 😎

Will my information be collected and stored?
Nope — we’re not paying for all that storage lol. Only the winner’s payout info is kept temporarily so the prize can be sent.
