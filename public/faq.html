FAQ

How does the game work?

Once per day, a 60-second entry window opens at a random time between 6-9 PM PST. During that minute, you can enter the draw. After the window closes, one winner is selected at random and receives $20.

How do I win?

Enter during the 60-second window. Speed does not matter. Everyone who enters during that minute has the same chance to win when the random draw happens.

What time does the drop happen?

The drop happens once daily at a random time between 6-9 PM PST.

Is it free?

Yes. No purchase necessary to enter or win.

Why do you give away $20 every day?

Why not? It is fun, chaotic, and gives someone a little cash daily.

Will my information be stored?

Basic claim information for entries and winners is stored so payouts can be verified and past winners can be tracked in the admin dashboard.
