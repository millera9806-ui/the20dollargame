Privacy Policy

Effective April 7, 2026

What We Collect

When you enter the draw, we may store the payout method you selected, the payout handle or identifier you submitted, your entry time, your IP address, and the round you entered. If you use live chat, we also store the message, message time, sender role, and IP address.

Why We Collect It

We use this information to run the game, prevent duplicate or fraudulent entries, pick and verify winners, send payouts, moderate live chat, maintain winner history, and protect the security of the site.

Third-Party Services

This site uses third-party tools such as hosting infrastructure and Google reCAPTCHA to help keep the game available and reduce spam or abuse. Those providers may process technical data needed to deliver their services.

How Long We Keep Data

Entry and winner records may be kept as long as reasonably necessary for payout verification, fraud prevention, bookkeeping, and business records. Live chat is moderated, may be removed at any time, and is generally kept only for a limited rolling period.

How We Share Data

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers that help operate the site, or when required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of the site, its users, or others.

Your Responsibility

Do not post sensitive personal or financial information in live chat. Messages that include contact details, payment requests, links, or abusive content may be blocked or removed.

Questions

For privacy questions, contact the20dollargame@gmail.com.
