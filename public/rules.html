Official Rules

Effective April 7, 2026

No Purchase Necessary

No purchase or payment is necessary to enter or win. A purchase will not improve your chances of winning.

Draw Schedule

The daily draw is intended to go live at 7:00 PM Pacific Time. The live entry window lasts 2 minutes once opened. The operator manually starts each round, so a draw may be delayed, modified, suspended, or canceled if needed.

How To Enter

Submit the required entry information through the site during the live entry window. Limit one entry per IP address per draw. Entries that are incomplete, duplicated, automated, deceptive, or otherwise abusive may be rejected or removed.

Winner Selection

One winner is selected at random from eligible entries received during that draw window after the timer expires.

Prize

The prize is twenty U.S. dollars ($20), paid through the payout method selected by the winner if payment can be completed successfully.

Verification And Disqualification

The operator may verify entries and may disqualify anyone suspected of fraud, duplicate entry farming, impersonation, abuse of the site or chat, attempts to bypass security measures, or violations of these rules.

Publicity And Sponsors

Winners may be announced by payout handle or username. Sponsor placements on the site are promotional placements only unless expressly stated otherwise.

Release And Limitations

By participating, you agree that the operator may administer the draw in its reasonable discretion and is not responsible for lost, late, incomplete, corrupted, misdirected, or technically interrupted entries, outages, or platform failures.

Void Where Prohibited

The draw is void where prohibited or restricted by law. You are responsible for ensuring that your participation is lawful in your jurisdiction.
